malvvani masala

In a large pan, we start with roasting byadagi red chilies.We must not brown the chilies but only roast them slightly till they release aroma. You can roast chilies in batches if roasting in large quantities. Roasting time: 4 minutes. Remove the chilies from Pan and keep it aside. Let's roast Pandi red chilies.We must not brown the chilies but only roast them slightly till they release aroma.Roasting time: 2 minutes.Remove the chilies from Pan and keep it aside.Let's roast other ingredients. Roast all the spices one by one.We will dry roast the ingredients in a smaller pan until the spices release aroma. Coriander seeds:Roasting time: 2 minutes Cumin seeds: Roasting time: 1 minute Peppercorns: Roasting time: 1 minute 30 seconds Black cumin seeds: Roasting time: 1 minute Mustard seeds: Roasting time:1 minute Fennel seeds: Roasting time: 1 minute 30 seconds Fenugreek seeds: Roasting time: 30 seconds Teppal: Roasting time: 1 minute Cloves: Roasting time: 1 minute Nagkesar: Roasting time: 1 minute Green cardamom: Roasting time: 1 minute Poppy seeds: Roasting time: 1 minute 30 seconds Star anise: Roasting time: 45 seconds Mugwort: Roasting time: 20 seconds Mace: Roasting time: 30 seconds Lichen(Stone flower): Roasting time: 45 seconds Bay leaf: Roasting time: 1 minute Cinnamon sticks: Roasting time: 1 minute 45 seconds Always crack open black cardamom before roasting.black cardamom: Roasting time: 1 minute 30 seconds Nutmeg must be coarsely powdered before roasting.Nutmeg: Roasting time: 10 seconds Turmeric root must be crushed before roasting.Turmeric root: Roasting time: 30 seconds Finally asafoetida rock must be fried.Heat some oil and turn off the flame.Now add asafoetida to the oil and fry until it changes its color.Frying time: 1-2 minutes.We will let all these spices cool down before we grind them together.Oil can be used for other recipes. As chilies cool down, grind them together.Remember we are not using ANY WATER. Let's grind other spices together.Remember we are not using ANY WATER. Finally combine both the powders and blend them together one last time. We have made 350 grams Malvani Masala.Malvani Masala can be used to make many Konkani recipes

Ingredients

200 grams dried Byadagi red chilies. These chilies add to the color of the masala. 50 grams dried Pandi chilies for spiciness 7 grams Cumin seeds 6 grams Teppal 7 grams Black cumin 9 grams Poppy seeds 5 grams fenugreek seeds 3 grams cloves 4 grams Nutmeg 7 grams Fennel seeds 6 grams whole coriander seeds 6 grams black cardamom 3 grams Star Anise 2 grams Mugwort 6 grams cinnamon stick 3 grams mace 10-12 Bay leaf 18 grams Turmeric root 2 grams lichen(black stone flower) 3 grams green cardamom 7 grams Nagkesar 7 grams asafoetida rock 5 grams Mustard seeds 4 grams peppercorns
